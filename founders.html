FAQ

Founder questions, answered

Everything founders need to know about raising capital with Launchpad. Have more questions? We're here to help.

Talk to Us
What stage of company is Launchpad right for?+

Launchpad supports founders from pre-seed through Series C. Our investor network and matching engine are calibrated for each stage, and your profile and matching criteria adapt to your current round size and metrics. We're most commonly used by companies raising $500K–$20M rounds.

How is Launchpad different from just using LinkedIn or cold email?+

Three key differences: (1) Investors on Launchpad are actively looking to invest — they've verified their availability and thesis. (2) Our AI matches you specifically by sector, stage, and portfolio fit — not generic keyword searches. (3) Every introduction is warm and double-opt-in, significantly increasing response rates compared to cold outreach.

How does the AI Pitch Coach work?+

Upload your pitch deck or connect your financial model. Our AI analyzes each component against a database of 5,000+ fundraising outcomes, identifies strengths and gaps, and gives specific suggestions for improvement. You can also use the chat interface to prepare for common investor questions and work through your narrative.

Is my company information kept confidential?+

Absolutely. Your information is only shared with investors you explicitly choose to connect with. Data room documents require your approval before access is granted. We're SOC 2 Type II certified and use enterprise-grade encryption throughout.

What does "active investor" mean on your platform?+

Active investors on Launchpad have been verified within the past 90 days and have confirmed they are actively deploying capital. We filter out investors who are "closed for new investments," in fundraising mode for their own fund, or haven't made an investment in 12+ months — ensuring your time is spent on genuinely active prospects.

How does the invitation process work?+

Submit your application in under 10 minutes. Our team reviews every application personally — typically within 3–5 business days. Approved founders receive a secure invitation link to activate their account. From activation, you can have your profile live and investor matches visible within 30 minutes.
