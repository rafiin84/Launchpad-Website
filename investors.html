FAQ

Investor questions, answered

Everything you need to know about getting started on Launchpad as an investor. Have more questions?

Book a Demo
How are startups vetted before appearing in deal flow?+

Every company goes through identity verification, business registration confirmation, financial data validation, and AI-powered traction analysis. We decline roughly 70% of companies that apply, ensuring only genuine, serious founders reach your feed.

Can I customize my deal flow filters and preferences?+

Absolutely. Set granular preferences including stage, sectors, geographies, check size range, business model type, and co-investor preferences. Your AI model also learns from your engagement behavior over time.

How does portfolio tracking work with existing investments?+

You can import your existing portfolio via CSV or connect directly to cap table software (Carta, Pulley). For existing portfolio companies on Launchpad, data syncs automatically. For others, you can manually input metrics or invite founders to submit updates.

Is Launchpad suitable for solo angels as well as VC funds?+

Yes — Launchpad serves everyone from solo angels writing $10K checks to multi-GP funds with $500M+ AUM. The platform scales to your needs with appropriate feature tiers for each investor type.

How does the LP reporting handle different fund structures?+

Our suite supports standard VC fund structures, SPVs, rolling funds, and scout programs. You can create multiple fund profiles under one account and generate separate reports for each. The system handles capital call schedules, distribution waterfalls, and management fee calculations automatically.
