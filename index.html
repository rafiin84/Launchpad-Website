How does Launchpad's investor matching work?+

Our AI analyzes your company profile across 200+ data points — including sector, stage, traction, team background, and growth trajectory — and cross-references this with each investor's stated thesis, portfolio history, and check size preferences. Matches are ranked by compatibility score, and improve over time as you interact with the platform.

Is my data secure and confidential?+

Absolutely. Launchpad is SOC 2 Type II certified and uses AES-256 encryption for all stored data. Your pitch materials and financials are only visible to investors you explicitly grant access to. We never share your data with third parties, and you can revoke access at any time from your dashboard.

Can I try Launchpad before paying?+

Yes! Our Free plan is available indefinitely — no credit card required. For paid plans, we offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. Enterprise plans include a complimentary proof-of-concept period.

What types of investors are on Launchpad?+

Our platform includes over 2,500 verified investors spanning venture capital firms, angel investors, family offices, and corporate venture arms. They range from pre-seed angels writing $25K checks to multi-stage VCs deploying $50M+. All investors are verified and actively looking for investments.

How long does it typically take to raise through Launchpad?+

Timelines vary by stage and market conditions, but founders on Launchpad close rounds 40% faster than industry benchmarks. Seed rounds typically close in 45–90 days; Series A in 90–180 days. Our pipeline management tools and AI coaching are specifically designed to accelerate the process at each stage.

Do you charge a success fee when a deal closes?+

No. Launchpad charges a flat subscription fee only. We never take a percentage of funds raised or deployed. Our incentive is entirely aligned with making the platform as valuable as possible — not skimming from your deal economics.
